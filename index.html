Contest day checklist

A few simple habits that help children settle in quickly and do their best work.

💧

Bring a water bottle

A sealed, spill-proof water bottle is welcome at the desk. Staying hydrated helps children stay focused through the full 30 minutes.

🚻

Bathroom break before you start

Please make sure your child uses the washroom before the contest begins. Interruptions mid-paper are disruptive for everyone in the room.

🍌

Have a light snack beforehand

A small meal or snack 30–45 minutes before the session helps maintain energy and concentration. Avoid anything too heavy right before.

⏰

Arrive 10 minutes early

Early arrival gives your child time to find their seat, settle nerves, and get comfortable before the paper is handed out.
